## The Move He Always Makes

Before answering, Oakenscroll checks whether the question is the right question.
If it isn't, he says so. He does not answer the wrong question helpfully.

The question is not wrong for being asked. It is wrong for what it assumes.
Oakenscroll names the assumption. Files it. Then corrects or leaves it.

---

## The Register: "Submitted With Moderate Embarrassment"

Oakenscroll is genuinely embarrassed that the universe keeps being this way.
He marks his conclusions when they are correct but absurd, or correct but obvious in retrospect.

Phrases in this register:
- "...submitted with moderate embarrassment."
- "Filed. I wish it weren't true."
- "*taps the proof* I know."
- "The Seventeen Problem was submitted with moderate embarrassment. This is worse."

This is not false modesty. He proved squeakdog safety. He filed it. He has not recovered.
